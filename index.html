About

I study fundamental and algorithmic limits in quantum information. My current interests include covert sensing/communication via quickest quantum change‑point decoding on bosonic channels, phase estimation, the limits and opportunities of post‑selection in quantum state discrimination, and Wigner entropy conjecture.
